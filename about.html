Lead Story

A Vancouver Analyst Making British Columbia's Public Data Legible — One Dataset at a Time

By Alx  ·  Vancouver, British Columbia  ·  Open to Analyst Roles

Vancouver-based analyst with a Master of Business Analytics from UBC has spent the better part of two years turning British Columbia's most complex public datasets into tools anyone can understand — dashboards that don't just display numbers, but tell the story behind them.

The work spans health authorities, crown corporations, school districts, transit agencies, and universities — drawing on Statement of Financial Information reports, BC Open Data portals, and the Vancouver Sun's public sector salary database to build intelligence tools for public accountability.

"The data exists. The question is whether anyone has made it legible."

Armed with Power BI, Tableau, D3.js, and Python, the analyst has built automated PDF extraction pipelines, vendor enrichment systems using the Claude API and BC Registry lookups, and interactive dashboards featuring drill-through profiles, spend segmentation, and anomaly detection.

Currently exploring the frontier of agentic AI in business intelligence — where language models, Python, and BI tools collaborate to produce insight without human intervention at every step.
